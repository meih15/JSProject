bobabreak

is a tea-themed matching game where the player serves as a barista in a bubble-tea shop newly opened in the bustling city of Los Angeles. Hone your barista skills as you take orders and serve customers the drink of their dreams!

Instructions

  • The player will take on the role of the barista that makes the bubble tea orders to serve the correct drink to the customer.
  • The player will be given three lives. A wrong order or order not completed in time will result in a customer lost, aka life lost.
  • Losing all three lives will result in a game over.

How to play

receive an order
click or type to choose correct item
click "Remove" or press backspace to deselect item
